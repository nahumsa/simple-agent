# FAQ

## What programming language should I use?

All challenges are language agnostic. Use whatever language you're most comfortable with, or use it as an opportunity to learn a new one.

## How long does each challenge take?

Each challenge is designed to be completable in less than 8 hours, though this varies by challenge complexity and your experience level.

## Do I need to be an experienced developer?

No. Challenges range from beginner to advanced. Start with a beginner challenge like the wc Tool and work your way up.

## Is the newsletter free?

Yes, the weekly coding challenge newsletter is free. There is an optional paid subscription at $8/month or $80/year that includes monthly AMA sessions, 20% off courses, and subscriber-only content.

## Are there courses available?

Yes. There are self-paced courses that walk you through specific challenges, and live courses where John Crickett personally teaches Go and systems programming.

## Where can I get help if I'm stuck?

First, try Googling the specific problem - most error messages and protocol questions have been answered before. If that doesn't help, ask a friend or colleague to talk it through with you. You can also join the Coding Challenges Discord community to ask other developers, or browse example solutions at https://github.com/CodingChallengesFYI/sharedsolutions to see how others approached the same challenge.
